Downtown Kigali Oasis

Located on KN 2 Ave in the heart of Kigali, Esta Café offers a quiet and comfortable escape from the downtown rush. Visitors praise its comfy seating, fast service, good vibes, and unique elevated views over Kigali Town.

Whether you are enjoying a cappuccino on the outdoor terrace, meeting friends, or taking a peaceful break in the city center, Esta Café turns a simple coffee stop into a calm Kigali moment.
